2023 4th FPT Education Conference on Business, Economics

2023 4th FPT Education Conference on Business, Economics and Management (FCBEM 2023) is organized to provide an interactive forum for academia, professors, researchers, scientists, lecturers and students to share and exchange new knowledge, innovations, and discuss past experiences and research findings with other participants. The conference welcomes participants in the region as well as those from different parts of the world who pay interest in doing research in business, economics and management and related topics. FCBEM 2023 also aims to promote research activities in FPT Education community and offers participants relationship-establishing and career opportunities with other attendants.
