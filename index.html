Introduction

FPT Education Conference on Business, Economics and Management (FCBEM) is an annual conference organized by The Office of Science Management and technically sponsored by one of the branches of FPT University. It is held annually to provide an interactive forum for presenters to share and exchange knowledge, innovations and research results in the fields of business, economics and management and other related fields.

Accepted and presented papers will be published by conference proceedings. Published papers are expected to be submitted for inclusion into EI Compendex, Scopus and other academic databases etc.

Review Policy

  • This review policy is followed for all academic papers submitted to conferences managed by the committee.
  • When the full paper is submitted, it is double-blind reviewed by a member of the conference committee. This review focuses on 10 issues intended to ensure that the paper is relevant to the conference, is of sufficiently high quality to be accepted for presentation at the conference and publication in the conference proceedings, and meets the submission criteria laid down by the program committee. A copy of the Review Form that is sent to reviewers can be seen here.
  • In the event that a reviewer rejects a paper, a second opinion will be taken, and the final decision about acceptance made by the conference committee.
  • The conference proceedings are produced in both printed and electronic form with ISBN numbers.
  • Submission dates can be found on the web page.
  • Some conferences include tracks with presentations from consultants, professionals, and vendors. These papers are accepted by the committee. However, a paper, as opposed to a set of PowerPoint slides, is nonetheless required.

Receiving Notification

After you received the formal notification letter, you can follow the steps below to finish registration.

  • If the paper cannot match the topics or is unqualified, a rejection email will be sent.
  • After the paper passes two rounds of the reviewing process, a formal acceptance letter will be sent, including the review form with comments, etc.
  • Please re-edit and improve paper quality according to the review comments carefully.

After Registration

  • Sending the final paper with Word and PDF, registration form, scanned payment proof to icess@sciei.org
  • Conference secretary will recheck your registration materials and submit the final version to our editor.
  • The editor will re-modify your paper format. If there are any problems, we will email you.
  • The secretary will confirm with you for final paper publication, guide you to fill copyright, and follow the other steps until publication successfully.
    Note: Please whitelist the conference mail address: icess@sciei.org. Checking your mailbox on time and making sure your phone is available.
